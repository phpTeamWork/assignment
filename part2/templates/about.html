About the Restaurant:

Hot Wok is a restaurant that serves a wide range of Chinese cuisine. Our restaurant is perfect for holding family dinners and events, also offering a take-away service for the island. We have been operating since May 2019 and would like to welcome you to try our delicious dishes!

Our Team:

Hot Wok is a family-run chinesese food restaurant, and the great team who will do their best to provide you with the best service on the island! The family has come all the way from China, bringing with them the best experience possible, whilst still having plenty years of experience in food retail on our island. Hot Wok is nothing without the family's passion, who are always ready to serve you.
